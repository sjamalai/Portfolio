// 001 — About

Innovation as a
discipline, not a
department.

Abundant Innovation (AI) is a solo-founder innovation studio built on one conviction: the most important problems in the world are open — and the best solutions come from those willing to start from zero.

Founded by a multi-disciplinary engineer with hands-on experience in aerospace, combustion, autonomous systems, and flight-critical software, AI participates in open innovation challenges where the stakes are highest and the orthodoxy thinnest.

We don't consult. We don't advise. We build, fabricate, ship, and compete.

// 003 — Approach

The method
behind the
madness

Open innovation isn't about incremental improvement. It's about rethinking the constraint set entirely. Every challenge we enter is treated as a system problem — not a product problem.

01

Challenge Identification

We scan HeroX, NASA, ESA, XPRIZE, and government bodies for open challenges where cross-disciplinary engineering creates an asymmetric advantage.

02

First-Principles Decomposition

Every problem is stripped to its physics, not its industry convention. What does the constraint actually require? What are we free to reimagine?

03

Rapid Prototyping & Fabrication

We build physical artifacts, not slide decks. CAD, machining, and material sourcing — wherever in the world it needs to happen.

04

Submission & Publication

Results are submitted to challenge organizers and published openly. Winning is the goal. Learning is the fallback.

05

IP Development

Winning concepts are developed into transferable intellectual property, licensing opportunities, or integrated into our own ventures.
