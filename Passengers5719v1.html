The Problem

Inefficient Propulsion Has Always Been the Norm.

Every vehicle on the road today moves the same way it did a century ago. A motor generates torque. A drivetrain transmits it. Losses stack up at every joint. The wheel — the only thing touching the ground — is purely passive. We asked: what if it didn't have to be?

01
Heavy & Complex

Traditional vehicles rely on motors, gearboxes, and drivetrains that add significant weight, cost, and failure points before a single watt reaches the road.

02
High Maintenance

More moving parts mean more wear, more repairs, and higher lifetime ownership costs — a burden borne by every rider, driver, and operator on earth.

03
Clunky Alternatives

Current e-mobility add-ons — external motors, battery packs, conversion kits — are cumbersome and inefficient. They add complexity rather than removing it.

How It Works

"The magic is in the mechanics."

Each spoke is precisely timed to generate forward thrust through coordinated extension and retraction. Advanced materials and unique articulation allow each spoke to act as both a structural element and a propulsion actuator — all within a single, elegant wheel. No separate motor. No separate drivetrain. One system.

1
◎
Spoke Engages

As each spoke reaches the power position, its compliant geometry locks and extends, making firm contact with the ground interface to initiate thrust.

2
⚡
Power Stroke

The engaged spoke transfers stored mechanical energy into a forward thrust vector, propelling the vehicle without any external motor input.

3
⇄
Sequential Handoff

The thrust cycle passes seamlessly from spoke to spoke around the wheel's circumference, ensuring continuous, smooth propulsion without gaps or pulses.

4
↻
Continuous Rotation

The full 54-spoke cycle completes continuously, delivering uninterrupted forward motion at any speed — powered entirely from within the wheel itself.

Key Benefits

◈
Reduced Complexity

Eliminates motors, gearboxes, and drive shafts entirely. The entire propulsion system lives inside the wheel — nothing external, nothing redundant.

◇
Lightweight Design

Dramatically reduces overall vehicle weight for better performance, longer range, and lower structural demands on the rest of the vehicle.

⚡
Enhanced Efficiency

Direct power transfer from spokes to motion — no energy lost to drivetrain friction, gear mesh losses, or belt slip. What goes in comes out as motion.

⚙
Lower Maintenance

Fewer moving parts means less wear, fewer repairs, and lower costs across the vehicle's lifetime. No motor windings to burn out. No gearbox oil to change. No belt to snap.

◎
Versatile Applications

Adaptable for e-bikes, electric wheelchairs, mobility scooters, and last-mile delivery vehicles. One core technology, multiple markets.
